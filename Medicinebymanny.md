# MedicineByManny.com

MedicineByManny is an online pharmacy that links patients and pharmacutical products directly through the web allowing patients to recive propet treatment at the comfort of their home

## problem statement

Due to illness or other factors patiens do not have the energy or willpower to go to the pharmacy to get the medicines they need for comfort and survival. Without any proxy to help with this task, many end up in a worse state than they even worse medicinebymanny helps solve this problem by reducing the phamacy to only a few clicks

## solution

MedicineByManny provides an online pharmacy platform that connects patients directly with pharmaceutical products and delivery services. The service simplifies ordering, prescription management, and home delivery so patients can receive proper treatment without leaving their home.

## objectives

- Make it easy for patients to order medicines online with a few clicks.
- Ensure safe and reliable delivery of pharmaceutical products.
- Reduce the burden on patients who cannot visit a pharmacy in person.
- Improve access to essential medications for patients facing illness or mobility challenges.
